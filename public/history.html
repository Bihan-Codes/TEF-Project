COMPANY HISTORY

Driven by the belief that education and skills are the strongest tools for empowerment, TEF continues to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements.

2018: Journey Started

TEF, formerly known as Neck Down Generation (NDG), was founded with a mission to generate employment and facilitate student placements through grassroots initiatives.

2020: 1st Speed Breaker

Amidst the COVID-19 pandemic, TEF expanded its focus to training, job placement, and career guidance, supporting individuals affected by job losses.

2023: Business Expansion

Established Mondal Learning Academy (MLA) to provide structured training programs, ensuring candidates are fully equipped before entering the workforce.

2024: TRIJAL Registered

Officially registered as Trijal Excelled Foundation, transitioning into a Section 8 company with a broader vision for national impact.

2024-25: Recognitions

Became a recognized training partner for PMKVY and PBSSD. Earned recognition as the 2nd best training center in West Bengal. Engaged with UNICEF, Infosys CSR, and IBM Skill Build Project.
