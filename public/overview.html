About Trijal Excelled Foundation

Trijal Excelled Foundation (TEF) is a purpose-driven Section 8 organization committed to empowering individuals and uplifting communities through skill development, education, and sustainable employment initiatives. Established in 2018 (formerly known as Neck Down Generation), TEF was founded with a mission to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements, ensuring youth are job-ready and self-reliant.

With its headquarters in West Bengal and operations across multiple states including Jharkhand, Odisha, Chhattisgarh, Bihar, and Assam, TEF has expanded its impact through strategic partnerships with government bodies, corporates, and training institutions. The organization is a recognized training partner under PMKVY and PBSSD and has successfully implemented national and CSR projects in collaboration with reputed organizations such as UNICEF, Infosys, IBM Skill Build, and others.

TEF’s core focus areas include:

  • Skill Development & Vocational Training
  • Employment Assistance & Job Placement
  • Women Empowerment Initiatives
  • Environmental Sustainability Programs
  • Humanitarian Support & Community Welfare

Through structured training programs, career guidance, and industry collaborations, TEF aims to create a skilled workforce that contributes to economic growth and social development. Guided by its vision of building a self-reliant society, TEF continues to drive meaningful change by providing accessible opportunities for education, livelihood, and community empowerment.
